Princess Nia's Coloring Book — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

TL;DR: Princess Nia's Coloring Book collects zero personal data. No tracking, no analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs, no accounts. Everything stays on your device.

Data We Collect

None. Princess Nia's Coloring Book does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from you, your device, or your child. We have no servers, no accounts, and no user tracking.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the following on your device only:

• In-progress coloring pages (so your child can continue where they left off)
• A small flag remembering that the welcome screen has been seen at least once

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes it permanently.

Photos Permission

The app requests permission to add photos only when your child taps the "Save to Photos" button. We use this permission to save the finished coloring page directly to the device's Photos library — nothing else. The app does not read existing photos, does not browse the photo library, and never uploads anything.

Third-Party Services

Princess Nia's Coloring Book does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No Google Analytics. No Facebook SDK. No ad networks. No crash reporting services.

Other Permissions

The app does not request any other device permissions. No camera, no microphone, no location, no contacts.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This app is designed for children. Since we collect zero data, there is nothing to protect under COPPA, GDPR-K, or any other children's privacy regulation. The app contains:

• No in-app purchases
• No ads
• No external links inside the coloring experience
• No way for users to communicate with anyone

In-App Purchases

Princess Nia's Coloring Book has no in-app purchases. The app is free, fully unlocked, with all coloring pages and tools available from day one.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update the "last updated" date above. Since we don't collect contact information, we can't notify you directly. You can always check this page for the latest version.
