How to Play

Goal: Defend the galaxy from waves of alien UFOs. Bounce your ball off your paddle to destroy enemy ships. Don't let your ball fall off the bottom.

Controls:

• Drag your finger to move the paddle left and right
• Tap anywhere to launch the ball from the paddle
• Hit targets, clear waves, survive bosses, chase high scores

Features

• Wave stages with 24+ unique ship formations
• Boss fights every 5 stages
• 5 power-ups: Multiball, Shield, Wide Paddle, Laser Ball, Slow Motion
• Heart pickups for extra lives
• Combo scoring system
• Ball and paddle customization

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Paddle Ship free?

Yes. Completely free. No in-app purchases, no ads, no subscriptions. Every feature is unlocked from the moment you install.

How do I pause the game?

Tap the pause button in the top-right corner of the screen during gameplay. From there you can access Settings, restart, or quit to the main menu.

I lost my high score!

High scores are saved on your device only. They are lost if you delete the app or if your device is restored from a backup that predates your high score.

Can I turn off the music or sound effects?

Yes. Pause the game, tap Settings, and toggle Sound Effects, Music, or Haptics individually.

Does the game work offline?

Yes. Paddle Ship has no network connection at all. It works entirely offline.

What devices does it run on?

iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.

Does it collect any data?

No. Paddle Ship has zero tracking, analytics, or data collection. See our Privacy Policy for details.
