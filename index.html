The DebtDrop Story

I saved $200,000 on my mortgage.
Then I built an app so you could too.

When I bought my first home, I started obsessively calculating how extra payments could shorten my loan. I built spreadsheets, modeled recast strategies, and ran the numbers over and over.

The result? I saved over $200,000 in interest. But the process was painful. The tools available were either confusing, inaccurate, or locked behind expensive subscriptions.

So I built DebtDrop, the app I wished I had from the beginning. A clean, powerful calculator that shows you exactly how every extra dollar impacts your loan. No guesswork. No paywalls.

Every competitor charges $5-15/month for a calculator. We give you more, for nothing.

About the Team

Real people building real tools

Nuvia Way was founded by Marc Julot, a former special education teacher turned software developer. With a Master's in Computer Information Systems and a Master's in Teaching, Marc brings a unique perspective: every app should be as accessible and intuitive as a well-designed lesson plan.

His wife Kasandra, a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA), consults on every app, ensuring the user experience is grounded in how people actually learn and make decisions.

Every app is tested by real families before it ships.
