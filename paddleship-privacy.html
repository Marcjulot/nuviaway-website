Paddle Ship Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

TL;DR: Paddle Ship collects zero personal data. No tracking, no analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs. Everything stays on your device.

Data We Collect

None. Paddle Ship does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from you or your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no user tracking.

Data Stored On Your Device

The game stores the following on your device only (using Apple's UserDefaults system):

• Your high score
• Your current stage progress
• Sound, music, and haptic preferences
• Tutorial/intro completion flags
• Ball and paddle color selection

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes it permanently.

Third-Party Services

Paddle Ship does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No Google Analytics. No Facebook SDK. No ad networks. No crash reporting services.

Permissions

Paddle Ship does not request any device permissions. No camera, no microphone, no location, no contacts, no photos.

Children's Privacy

Paddle Ship is safe for all ages. Since we don't collect any data, there are no special protections needed for children. The game contains no in-app purchases, no ads, no links to external websites, and no way to communicate with other users.

In-App Purchases

Paddle Ship has no in-app purchases. The game is completely free, fully unlocked, with all features available from day one.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update the "last updated" date above. Since we don't collect contact information, we can't notify you directly. You can always check this page for the latest version.
