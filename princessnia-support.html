How to Color

Pick a page from the gallery — Animals, Vehicles, or School Supplies. Then:

• Tap a region with the bucket tool to fill it with color
• Drag with the brush tool to paint freely
• Pinch with two fingers to zoom in for detail (1× to 4×)
• Two-finger drag to scroll while zoomed
• Tap the undo arrow to step back
• Tap the ⋯ menu to start over or save the page to Photos

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes. Completely free. No in-app purchases, no ads, no subscriptions. Every coloring page and color is unlocked from the moment you install.

Will my child's progress be saved?

Yes. Each page autosaves as your child colors. They can come back to a half-finished page anytime.

How do I save a finished page?

Tap the ⋯ button in the top-right while coloring, then pick Save to Photos. The first time you do this, iOS will ask permission to add photos — that's only used to save the picture.

How do I start over on a page?

Tap the ⋯ button while coloring and pick Start Over. This erases all colors on that page and restores the line art.

My child colored outside the lines / a fill leaked.

Tap the undo arrow at the top to step back. Each fill or brush stroke is one undo step (up to 20 in a row).

Does the app work offline?

Yes. The app has no network connection. It works entirely offline.

What devices does it run on?

iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.

Does it collect any data?

No. The app has zero tracking, analytics, or data collection. See our Privacy Policy for details.
